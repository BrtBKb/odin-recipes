Doritos Ramen

Mix beef and ramen noodles. On a plate, place doritos to make a "bed" for the beef mixture. Place beef mixture on top of doritos and top with cheddar cheese.

Ingredients

  • A bag of lime chili Doritos.
  • A bag of Ramen Noodles, Beef Flavor.
  • A pack of Sazon
  • A pack of Goya
  • Cheddar cheese

Steps:

  • Step 1:Boil a pot of water.
  • Step 2:Mix the bag of Ramen Noodles into the pot.
  • Step 3:When ready, mix all the seasoning when the water is boiling. Add one egg for extra flavor.
  • Step 4:Pour into your favorite bowl.
  • Step 6:Top with crushed Doritos and add cheedar cheese over it.
