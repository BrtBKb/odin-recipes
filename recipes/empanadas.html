Empanadas

An empanada is a pastry, akin to a turnover, popular in Latin America, that originated in Spain. The pastry is usually filled with a cheese and meat and wrapped with a flaky dough and fried or baked.

Ingredients

  • Frozen Goya Empanada
  • Tropicana White Cheese
  • 1/2 of Honey Ham
  • Avacado Oil
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Step 1:Defrosrt frozen empanada.
  2. Step 2:Dice cheese and ham into cubes.
  3. Step 3:Pre-heat pan filled with a good amount of oil to deep fry the empanada.
  4. Step 4:Put the dice ham and cheese onto the empanada disc.
  5. Step 5:After enclosing the empanadas, deep fry them into the preheated pan.
  6. Step 6:Allow empanadas to dry up. Toss a pinch a salt ontop of the empanadas. Enjoy.
